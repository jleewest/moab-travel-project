Why Moab?

Moab is located in the Southeast corner of Utah, on the ancestral homeland of the Ute (Noochew), Navajo (Dineh), Paiute (Nuwuvi), and Hopi (Hisatsinom) people.

From scenic rivers and forests to thousands of square miles of ruggedly beautiful land, the town of Moab is surrounded by natural wonders. Combining small-town hospitality with unparalleled natural beauty and the cool waters of the Colorado River, Moab has become one of the most sought-after destinations in the American Southwest.
